Frequently Asked Questions About Pool renovation

How much does it typically cost to renovate a plunge pool on the Gold Coast?
The cost to renovate a plunge pool on the Gold Coast usually ranges from $15,000 to $40,000, depending on the size of your pool, the materials chosen (such as tiles, pebblecrete, or glass bead finishes), and the extent of the renovation. If you’re after additional features like new lighting, heating, or water features, these can increase the price. We offer detailed on-site assessments to provide an accurate, itemized quote tailored to your needs and property.
How long does a typical plunge pool renovation take from start to finish?
Most plunge pool renovations on the Gold Coast are completed within 3 to 6 weeks, depending on the complexity of the project and weather conditions. The process includes draining and prepping the pool, carrying out structural repairs if needed, resurfacing, installing new fittings, and refilling and balancing the water. We'll provide you with a detailed timeline before starting, and keep you updated throughout the project.
Are you licensed and insured to carry out pool renovations in Queensland?
Yes, Plunge Pools Gold Coast is fully licensed with the Queensland Building and Construction Commission (QBCC) and holds all required insurances for pool renovations. Our team stays up-to-date with all local Gold Coast and Queensland regulations, so you can have peace of mind knowing your renovation meets all legal and safety standards.
What does your plunge pool renovation process involve?
Our process starts with an on-site inspection of your existing plunge pool to assess its condition and discuss your renovation goals. We then provide a detailed quote and project plan. Once approved, we handle draining the pool, repairing any structural issues, resurfacing with your choice of finish, updating plumbing and electrical components if needed, and installing any new features. After completion, we refill and balance the water, and walk you through the finished result.
Do you offer any warranties or guarantees on your pool renovation work?
Yes, all our renovation work comes with a workmanship guarantee, and the materials we use are backed by manufacturer warranties—usually ranging from 5 to 10 years depending on the product. We’ll provide you with all warranty documentation upon completion and are always available for any follow-up support or questions you may have after your renovation.
